# March Madness Vibe Checker

What games are worth watching in the NCAA Tournament? We see all the stats and the brackets — but let's be real. Few of us actually watch enough hoops to make sense of the ESPN and KenPom analytics. We casual fans are in it for the vibes. Which teams are heating up right now? Who can pull off an upset? Which games are worth watching? 

This tool is designed to help you get a feel for the field, and know who to watch.
